# Product Guidelines

## Tone & Prose Style
- **Professional & Direct**: The documentation and UI messaging should be clear, concise, and focused strictly on the technical information to aid rapid troubleshooting.

## Visual Branding & UI
- **Minimalist & Clean**: The aesthetic should focus on typography and whitespace with a muted color palette, allowing the service status indicators (e.g., emojis) to stand out clearly.

## UX Principles
- **Discoverability First**: All features and statuses should be immediately visible or easy to find without deep navigation.
- **Configuration as Code**: Emphasize YAML/Env configuration over complex UI settings menus to align with a zero-friction deployment goal.
- **Real-Time Feedback**: Immediate visual cues (e.g., emojis) and smooth transitions for state changes.

## Accessibility & Inclusivity
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Full support for navigating the SPA without a mouse.
- **Clear Language**: Avoid jargon where possible and provide tooltips for technical terms to ensure clarity for all developers.
